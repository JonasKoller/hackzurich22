WELCOME TO AQUATRICITY

Thank you for checking out our app.

The goal of this app is to inform you about sustainable behaviour and to give you easy to achieve tips and tricks to save energy, water and other valuable resources.

We are sure you will find something interesting and new for you in our app. Happy exploring!

WHAT ARE WE ABOUT

The Aquatricity app was created at HackZurich 2022. So right now the features are limited, but the sky is the limit.

Our goal is to adapt Neil Amstrong's "one small step for a man - one giant leap for mankind" to the future of sustainability. Each and everyone can contribute to a better future - especially in times of crisis like we are facing now.

We want to move as many people as possible a step into the direction of environmental responsibility and sensible consumption to achieve a big change.
